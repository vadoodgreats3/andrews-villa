How we use cookies and similar technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Session Cookies Keep you logged in during your visit, maintain shopping cart items, enable secure payments Session
Essential Authentication Cookies Remember your login status and preferences for property searches 30 days
Analytics Google Analytics Understand how users interact with our site to improve user experience 2 years
Functional Preference Cookies Remember your language, currency (including crypto preferences), and display settings 1 year
Functional Payment Cookies Remember your preferred payment method (Cash App, crypto, Chime) for faster checkout 90 days

Payment & Crypto Transaction Cookies

When processing payments, including cryptocurrency transactions, we use specialized cookies to:

  • Securely store your payment method preferences
  • Remember your crypto wallet addresses (encrypted and hashed)
  • Maintain transaction security during crypto payment processing
  • Prevent fraud in cryptocurrency transactions
  • Remember your preferred currency display (USD, BTC, ETH, etc.)

Security Note

We never store sensitive information like private keys, seed phrases, or full payment details in cookies. All sensitive crypto transaction data is encrypted and stored securely on our servers.

Third-Party Cookies

We use trusted third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • Cloudinary: For property image optimization and delivery
  • Payment Processors: For secure payment handling (including crypto gateways)
  • Analytics Services: To understand user behavior and improve our platform
  • Chat Services: For real-time communication between users and admins

Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and data stored

Note: Disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of Andrews Villa, including property bookings, saved properties, and payment processing.
